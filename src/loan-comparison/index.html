Owning a Home BETA

Explore loan scenarios and compare estimated costs

The interest and fees you pay to borrow money to buy a home can often be as much, or more, as the purchase price of the home itself. That's why it's important to think carefully about which loan option is right for you and shop around to get the best deal. The loan type you choose, your down payment amount, and the interest rate you receive all can have a big impact on your total cost. Use this tool to explore different loan scenarios and determine your best strategy.

About our data source

Our data is provided by real lenders and is updated daily. The lenders in our data include a mix of large banks, regional banks, and credit unions.

The data is provided by Informa Research Services, Inc., Calabasas, CA. www.informars.com. Informa collects the data directly from lenders and every effort is made to collect the most accurate data possible, but they cannot guarantee the data’s accuracy.
