What happens next

You’re in control

Once you’re ready, you need tell the lender you want to proceed with a particular loan offer. At that point, the lender will typically charge you an application and appraisal fee and request additional documentation like income statements and a list of assets. If you think you might need more than 10 days to decide to proceed with a loan offer, it’s a good idea to advise the loan officer so they don't close your loan application.

Learn more about intent to proceed

You might receive revised Loan Estimates if important numbers change

If there are changes in your application—including your loan amount, credit score, or verified income—your rate and terms will probably change too. In those situations, the lender will give you a revised Loan Estimate. If your Loan Estimate is revised you should look it over to see what changed. Ask your lender: “Can you explain why I received a new Loan Estimate?” and “How is my loan transaction different from what I was originally expecting?”
